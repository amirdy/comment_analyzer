Welcome
This Project is called Comment Analyzer which is a sentiment analysis project.
  1. Consider a product in your mind
  2. Leave your comment about that.
  3. See the result.
What is the result:

The result indicates whether your comment on the product was Positive or Negative (with their probabilities). It shows whether you are Satisfied with the product or Not.
